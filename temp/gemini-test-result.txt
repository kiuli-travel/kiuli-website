Escape to unparalleled luxury and create unforgettable memories at the One&Only Cape Town, the ultimate destination for an **exclusive luxury safari** experience with a sophisticated city twist. Nestled against the iconic backdrop of Table Mountain, this world-class resort offers an idyllic blend of natural beauty and refined comfort, providing a sanctuary for discerning travelers seeking an extraordinary African adventure.

Imagine waking up to breathtaking vistas from your spacious, meticulously appointed room or suite. Each accommodation is designed to maximize your enjoyment of the stunning surroundings, offering sweeping views of either the vibrant marina or the majestic Table Mountain. Indulge in the plush furnishings, state-of-the-art amenities, and personalized service that define the One&Only experience.

Beyond the luxurious accommodations, the One&Only Cape Town is a culinary paradise. Embark on a gastronomic journey at Nobu, one of the world's most renowned restaurants, where innovative Japanese cuisine tantalizes your taste buds. Explore a variety of other exceptional dining and bar options, including Isola, offering contemporary Italian cuisine with a South African flair, and Vista Bar & Lounge, a relaxed and stylish venue perfect for enjoying handcrafted cocktails and light bites while soaking in the panoramic views.

For ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation, the award-winning One&Only Spa awaits. Immerse yourself in a world of tranquility with a range of holistic treatments and therapies designed to revitalize your body, mind, and soul. From invigorating massages to personalized facials, the expert therapists will tailor each experience to your individual needs.

While offering an **exclusive** urban escape, the One&Only Cape Town also serves as the perfect base for exploring the wonders of South Africa. Its prime location provides easy access to Cape Town's vibrant attractions, including the nearby V&A Waterfront, where you can embark on thrilling **luxury safari** day trips to nearby game reserves. Witness the "Big Five" in their natural habitat and experience the magic of the African bush, returning to the luxurious comfort of the resort each evening.

Families traveling with children will appreciate the dedicated KidsOnly programme, offering a range of fun, supervised activities designed to entertain and engage younger guests. Tailored experiences for older kids ensure that everyone enjoys a memorable and enriching vacation.

**INSIDER'S TIP:** Elevate your **luxury safari** experience by booking a private cabana by the pool for the day. This **exclusive** retreat offers the perfect combination of privacy and indulgence, complete with attentive service and unforgettable views of Table Mountain. Create lasting memories as you bask in the South African sunshine and enjoy the unparalleled luxury of the One&Only Cape Town.
