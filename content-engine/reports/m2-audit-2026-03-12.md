### Issue 1: All itineraries are draft status

The safaris page currently renders all 5 because it uses `overrideAccess: true` without filtering by `_status: 'published'`. This is a dev convenience — at launch, either the itineraries need to be published, or the query intentionally shows drafts. Currently no itinerary has been reviewed/enhanced/published through the editorial workflow.

### Issue 2: 3 of 4 published articles have no hero images

Posts 22, 23, 24 are published but have `hero_image_id = NULL`. The articles listing page filters these out (`if (!heroImage) return null`). Only post 25 has a hero image and appears on the site.

### Step 1: Assign hero images to the 3 published articles

### Step 2: Draft and publish destination pages
19 destination_page ContentProjects at stage='idea'. Each needs:
1. Click "Draft" → content engine drafts 9 sections using BrandVoice
2. Review sections
3. Click "Publish" → publisher writes sections to destination record, sets _status='published'

For the 10 country-level destinations, the same process applies (cascade may not have created projects for countries — need to verify).

### Step 3: Draft and publish property pages

3. Click "Publish"
